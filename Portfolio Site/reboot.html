Research Phase

  • Who: Targets induviduals and businesses seeking renewed electronics and companies refurbishing them.
  • What: A dedicated platform facilitating the buying and selling of refurbished electronics.
  • Where: Globally accessible via iOS and Android devices.
  • When: Operates 24/7 to accommodate shopping needs.
  • Why: Establishes a reliable and affordable marketplace for refurbishedelectronics.

Design Phase

1. Multi-Platform Design

Crafted with adaptability in mind, ReBoot is developed for both iOS and Android platforms, ensuring a broader reach and user base. The design process strictly followed the guidelines set by both iOS and Material Design, ensuring a seamless and standardized user experience.

2. Simplicity and Clarity

The design emphasizes straightforward and clear navigation, enabling users to effortlessly search for products, view details, and interact with seller profiles.

User Testing and Feedback

User testing was conducted to garner critical feedback, shedding light on the strengths and areas for further refinement in the design:

Positive Feedback

Users admired various elements, praising the easy navigation, effective use of color contrast, and other features enhancing the user experience.

Constructive Feedback

Feedback pointed towards improvements such as a prominent display of the app's name on the landing screen and detailed product page information.

Implementation

Responsive Design

The app was designed to be responsive, ensuring smooth operation across different devices on iOS and Android platforms.

Loading Speed

Graphics were optimized to promote quick loading times, enhancing user satisfaction, especially for those seeking quick purchases.
